Networking Assistant for LinkedIn — Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 20, 2026
Provided by: JeannetDevS
Contact: chromedev.scientist481@slmails.com

Networking Assistant for LinkedIn ("the extension") is a browser extension that helps coaching clients work their networking on LinkedIn using their own coaching workbook (a Google Sheet). This policy explains what data the extension accesses, what it sends off your device, and your choices.

Data the extension accesses on your device

  • Your Google Sheets coaching workbook. With your permission (Google OAuth, spreadsheets scope only), the extension reads your chosen workbook and writes to its "CALL TRACKER" tab when you click "Add to Call Tracker." It requests no Google Drive access — you choose the workbook by pasting its link.
  • Calendly (optional). If you connect Calendly, the extension reads your scheduled events to show upcoming meetings.
  • LinkedIn pages you view. While you're on LinkedIn, the extension reads the current page (e.g. a profile's name and public handle, or a job's company) to match against your workbook. This matching happens locally in your browser; this page content is not sent to our servers.

OAuth tokens, your chosen workbook ID, cached workbook data, and your settings are stored only in your browser's local storage (chrome.storage.local) on your device. You can clear it any time by removing the extension.

Data the extension sends to our servers

We operate one backend (Supabase) that we own and control — not a third-party analytics provider. Two things are sent there:

  1. Anonymous usage telemetry (on by default; you can opt out). To understand which features work and catch errors, the extension sends anonymous events: event names, coarse categories, booleans, counts, durations, error codes, app version, browser, locale, and a random install identifier that is not tied to you, your Google account, or your workbook. It is PII-free by construction, and our server additionally drops any unexpected fields. We do not send: your name, anyone's name, profile links, message text, company or workbook names, sheet IDs, tokens, or email addresses. Turn it off any time under Settings → Privacy.
  2. Feedback you choose to send. If you use the Settings feedback box, the text you type is sent to us along with the anonymous install identifier and app version. This is free text you author — please include personal details (like your email) only if you want to. You may optionally paste a screenshot; if you do, it is sent and stored with your feedback. Screenshots are entirely optional and only included when you paste one — please avoid capturing other people's personal information you don't intend to share.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell or rent your data.
  • We do not use third-party advertising or analytics SDKs.
  • We do not collect your browsing history or activity outside of LinkedIn and the Google/Calendly connections you authorize.

Third parties

  • Google and Calendly receive requests directly from your browser under the access you grant; your use of them is governed by their own privacy policies.
  • Supabase hosts our backend on our behalf as our infrastructure provider.

Data retention

Anonymous telemetry is retained for 365 days and then deleted or aggregated. Feedback is retained until we've acted on it. You can request deletion of feedback you sent by contacting us at chromedev.scientist481@slmails.com.

Children

The extension is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your jurisdiction) and we do not knowingly collect their data.

Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected here with a new effective date.
